In Faith, Values, and Learning (IFVL)

“Let the wise hear and increase in learning, and the one who understands obtain guidance.” — Proverbs 1:5 (ESV)

Learning is not only about information; it is also about formation. IFVL highlights how faith-informed values — diligence, humility, integrity, and compassion — shape the way we study, teach, and serve others. As we pursue knowledge, we also pursue wisdom that builds character and benefits our community.

May our growth be guided by understanding and aligned with what is true and good, so that learning transforms both our minds and our lives.
