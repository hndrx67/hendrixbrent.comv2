Learning Objectives

01

Recognize Basic Units

Recognize the basic units of morphology (morphemes) and separate the differences of free and bound morphemes.

02

Break Down Word Structure

Break down the structure of words by breaking them into roots, prefixes, and suffixes.

03

Apply Morphological Rules

Use the knowledge of morphological rules to construct and interpret new or unfamiliar words.
