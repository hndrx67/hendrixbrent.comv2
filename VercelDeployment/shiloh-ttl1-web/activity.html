Activity

Morphology Table Challenge

Instructions:

  1. 1 Form groups.
  2. 2 Each group sends one member to the "Morphology Table."
  3. 3 The member will draw one root word and one or more affixes (prefixes or suffixes).
  4. 4 Work together to form a correctly spelled and meaningful word. You may rearrange the affixes but cannot change the root word.
  5. 5 Write the word on the board and explain its meaning in less than 30 seconds before the wind carries it away!
