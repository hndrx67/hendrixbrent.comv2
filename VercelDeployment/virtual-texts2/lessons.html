Learn the Secrets of Visual & Virtual Texts

Explore bright examples, spot clues, and become a text detective.

What Are Visual Texts?

Visual texts are images, posters, comics, and infographics. They use color, layout, and pictures to tell stories without lots of words.

  • Look for captions and labels.
  • Notice colors and shapes.
  • Ask: What do I feel or learn?

What Are Virtual Texts?

Virtual texts live on screens! Think of websites, games, or interactive stories that let you click, watch, and explore.

  • Follow buttons and links.
  • Watch for animations.
  • Check who made it and why.

Explorer Skills

Super explorers use powerful strategies to understand any text.

  • Scan the whole scene first.
  • Zoom in on important clues.
  • Connect it with what you already know.
