  • Top-Left First: We naturally look here first

  • White Space: Empty areas help focus attention

ğŸ”¬ Critical Thinking Questions:

  • Audience: Who is this made for? How can you tell?
  • Purpose: Does it want to sell, teach, entertain, or convince?
  • Bias: What viewpoint does it show? What's left out?
  • Evidence: Are there facts, opinions, or just pretty pictures?
  • Credibility: Who created it? Can you trust the source?
  • Context: When and where was this made? Does that matter?
