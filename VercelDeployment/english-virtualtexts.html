What is a Virtual/Visual Text?

A virtual text lives on a screen. It can include words, pictures, buttons, links, audio, and video. You can usually click or tap parts of it to do things‚Äîlike open a new page, watch a clip, or zoom an image.

Spot the Features

  • Links take you to another page when clicked.
  • Images help you picture ideas quickly.
  • Headings show the topic of a section.
  • Captions tell what a picture or video shows.
  • Buttons let you do actions (play, pause, next).
  • Video shows moving pictures and sound.
