Principles

Simple practices that go a long way toward keeping your identity and activity private.

Minimize exposure

Share less. Use burner emails, compartmentalize accounts, and avoid reusing identifiers.

Encrypt everything

Prefer end‑to‑end encryption for chat, storage, and backups. Use full-disk encryption on devices.

Control your network

Use trusted DNS, privacy‑respecting browsers, and consider VPN/Tor where appropriate.

Starter toolkit

A short list of categories to explore. Choose reputable, open, and audited options when possible.

Browsers

Hardened privacy browsers with tracker blocking and containerization help isolate your identities.

Secure chat

Prefer E2EE messengers with safety numbers and sealed sender to reduce metadata.

Passwords

Use a password manager with unique, long passwords and enable multi‑factor authentication.
