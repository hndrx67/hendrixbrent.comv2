About Us

Kultura Virtual is a homegrown Vtuber agency based in the Philippines, celebrating Filipino stories, voices, and charm in the digital realm. We uplift underrepresented voices through high-quality Vtuber training, production, and streaming support.

Founded with a vision to blend our rich cultural heritage with cutting-edge digital entertainment, we're creating a space where Filipino creativity can flourish in the virtual world. Our talents bring authentic Filipino experiences to global audiences while staying true to our roots.

"We created Kultura Virtual with the dream of seeing Filipino stories take center stage in the VTuber world. Each of our talents carries a piece of our beautiful culture with them into the virtual realm, sharing our heritage, humor, and heart with the world."

- The Founder

Our Cultural Pillars

Bayanihan Spirit

Unity through Collaboration

We foster a community where our talents support each other, collaborate creatively, and lift each other up, just as neighbors come together to move a house in the traditional bayanihan.

Pagmamalasakit

Deep Care for Fans

We cherish genuine connections with our community, showing sincere care and consideration in every interaction, creating a supportive space where everyone feels valued.

Malikhaing Kaluluwa

Creative Soul

We embrace the innovative spirit that flows through Filipino art, music, and storytelling, encouraging our talents to explore their creative boundaries while honoring our cultural roots.
